2. Sensitive personal information (CPRA)

The main item of sensitive personal information we handle is your account log-in credentials (email + password, or Sign in with Apple), which we use only to authenticate you and secure your account. Users may also voluntarily reveal sensitive details in free-text or photos they post; we do not require this and do not use it to infer characteristics.

We use sensitive personal information only for the permitted purposes under the CPRA (such as providing the service you requested and securing it) and not to infer characteristics. Accordingly, the “Right to Limit the Use of Sensitive Personal Information” is offered as described in the Privacy Policy, though our uses fall within the exemptions that do not trigger a mandatory limit option.

7. International data transfers

Personal information may be processed outside the EEA/UK, including in the United States, by Supabase and Apple. Our primary hosting region is ap-northeast-1. For transfers out of the EEA/UK we rely on appropriate safeguards, including the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and the UK International Data Transfer Addendum, plus any supplementary measures required as applicable. Copies of relevant safeguards are available on request at charlx.studio@gmail.com.

9. Security measures

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption in transit; encryption at rest via the hosting provider.
  • Credentials handled by the auth provider with industry-standard hashing; tokens in the iOS Keychain.
  • Access controls and least-privilege, including database access controls provided by our hosting platform.
  • On-device photo downscaling/compression before upload.
  • Encrypted backups via our hosting provider, and reliance on the security practices of our providers (Supabase and Apple).
